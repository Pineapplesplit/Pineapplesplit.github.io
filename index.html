About Panda Pens

My name is Preston and I have decided to begin making and selling wood products such as pens, pencils, and pen cases. I came up with Panda Pens from taking the woodshop class at my school and have since been fascinated with making things with the lathe. My dad helped me save up for my first lathe and we've started a business together selling pens. Panda Pens happens to stand for Preston and Papa (P AND A). I hope you are happy with the pens you get, thank you for considering Panda Pens. Have a fantastic day!
